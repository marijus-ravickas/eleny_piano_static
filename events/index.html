Concerts, open lessons, and recitals

Events

Performance practice concerts happen most months — a low-stakes stage for students to practice performing — building up to two bigger moments in the year: the winter and spring recitals. The year also includes two off-site trips, in Mallorca and Vienna, open by invitation to participating students.
