Founder & Teacher, Piano

Elena Šešelgytė

Elena Šešelgytė is a concert pianist and the founder of Eleny Piano Studio, teaching piano since 2012.

She holds a Bachelor’s and Master’s degree in Music from the Royal Conservatory of Brussels, where she studied under pianist Prof. Daniel Blumenthal, with further study in Vienna and Belgium. In 2024 she completed a second Master’s degree at Vytautas Magnus University, under Prof. Alexander Paley.

Elena has performed internationally — in Spain, Belgium, France, the USA, and Lithuania — across solo recitals, collaborations with symphonic orchestras, and chamber music projects. It’s this same performing background that shapes the studio’s own chamber music and recital offerings for students.

Elena created the “Eleny Piano” method — a teaching approach combining a holistic perspective, breathing techniques, mindfulness practices, and body awareness, aimed at a deeper connection with music and with oneself.
