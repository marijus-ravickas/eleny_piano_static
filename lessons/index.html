How lessons work

Lessons

Individual and small-group lessons in piano, violin/viola, and vocal (coming soon) — in the studio or at home.

Individual
One-on-one, paced to the student.
Group Theory
Max 3 students — ear training and note-reading. Own keyboards provided, small enough to stay personal.
Custom, in-studio
A flexible format built around a specific goal.
At your home
Lessons brought to the student's own instrument.

Most students learn one-on-one, but Group Theory sits alongside individual lessons deliberately: peer learning and a social element, while staying small enough — max 3 students — to still be personal rather than a class.
